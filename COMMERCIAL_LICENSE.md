## When Do You Need a Commercial License?

A paid Commercial License is required if you or your organization fits any of the following profiles:

1. **Companies & Startups**: Any use of AssetFlow CLI in commercial software development, local workflows, or deployment pipelines by a company, corporation, startup, or for-profit entity.
2. **Agencies & Consultants**: Any use of AssetFlow CLI by an agency or consultant to optimize assets for clients or projects that generate revenue.
3. **Internal Business Use**: Running the CLI within any business environment, internal tool stack, or intranet systems to optimize corporate assets.
4. **SaaS & Hosted Services**: Integrating AssetFlow CLI into a software-as-a-service (SaaS) product, platform-as-a-service (PaaS), hosted website builder, or similar offering.
5. **Redistribution & White-Labeling**: Distributing AssetFlow CLI or any derivative work as part of another product, bundling it in a development tool, or rebranding/white-labeling the CLI.
6. **OEM Integrations**: Embedding AssetFlow CLI inside hardware, IDE plugins, commercial packages, desktop tools, or other software systems.

AssetFlow reserves all commercial rights exclusively.

## Commercial Licensing Tiers (Future Structure)

To accommodate different organizational scales, AssetFlow offers the following commercial tiers:

- **Pro Edition**: Designed for individual developers, agencies, and small teams. Includes advanced features, email support, and individual developer seating.
- **Enterprise Edition**: Designed for larger teams and corporations. Includes multi-seat organization licenses, priority SLA support, offline/on-premise activations, SSO, update entitlements, and custom configuration reviews.
