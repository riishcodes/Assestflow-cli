# Trademark Guidelines

This document details the trademark policy for the AssetFlow brand. We want to prevent user confusion, protect our identity, and ensure the community can trust official releases.

## AssetFlow Trademark and Intellectual Property

The names **AssetFlow**, **AssetFlow CLI**, and the official logo, icons, visual style, trade dress, website layout, and marketing assets are protected trademarks and intellectual property of AssetFlow.

## Trademark Policy Details

1. **No Automatic Rights**: The source-available AssetFlow Community License does **not** grant you any rights to use AssetFlow trademarks, logos, branding, or website assets.
2. **Name Protection**: You may not rename, rebrand, or publish derivative versions of the Software using the name "AssetFlow" or any name confusingly similar (e.g., "AssetFlow-Pro", "Asset-Flow").
3. **Asset & Logo Protection**: The official logos, branding assets, terminal banners, icons, website graphics, and layout designs cannot be reused, copied, or redistribution as part of any third-party fork, website, product, or package without explicit, prior written permission.
4. **Referential Use**: You may make referential statements to AssetFlow (e.g., "This project integrates with AssetFlow CLI") provided that:
   - You do not imply endorsement, sponsorship, or affiliation with AssetFlow.
   - The reference is factual and does not mislead users.
   - The AssetFlow trademark is not the most prominent name or branding element of your project.
